Golden Helix
Date: July, 09, 2014
---------------------

1) A. thaliana data overview:

- downloaded data from Gregor Mendel Institute
- custom affymetrix 250K SNP chip
- Genotyped 1307 samples; Covering 214,051 markers.
- Analysed data was A.thaliana affected by Pseudomonas.

2) Raw Data

- haploid genotypes
- homozygous diploid genotypes
- Ref. genome of thliana was downloaded form arabidopsis.org
